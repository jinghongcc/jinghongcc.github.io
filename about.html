About Jinghong

Jinghong (HongKong) Technology Limited

Leading the future of short-form entertainment

Our Story

Jinghong (HongKong) Technology Limited is a forward-thinking technology company specializing in the development and distribution of premium short drama content. Founded with a vision to revolutionize how audiences consume entertainment, we combine cutting-edge technology with compelling storytelling to deliver an unparalleled viewing experience.

In an era where attention spans are shorter and content consumption patterns are evolving, we recognized the growing demand for high-quality, bite-sized entertainment. Our platform brings together the best short dramas from around the world, making premium content accessible to audiences everywhere.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to premium short-form entertainment while supporting creators and storytellers worldwide. We believe that great stories shouldn't be limited by format or duration, and that every moment of entertainment should be meaningful and engaging.

Our Values

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of technology to enhance the viewing experience and support content creators.

Quality

We curate only the finest content, ensuring that every piece meets our high standards for storytelling and production.

User-Centric

Our users are at the heart of everything we do. We design our platform with their needs and preferences in mind.

Global Perspective

We celebrate diversity and bring together stories from different cultures, languages, and perspectives.

What We Do

Jinghong Technology operates a state-of-the-art short drama streaming platform that offers users access to a vast library of premium short-form content. Our platform features:

  • A curated selection of high-quality short dramas across multiple genres
  • Advanced streaming technology for seamless playback
  • Personalized recommendations powered by AI
  • Multi-device support for viewing anywhere, anytime
  • Support for multiple languages and subtitles

Our Commitment

At Jinghong Technology, we are committed to providing a safe, secure, and enjoyable platform for both content creators and viewers. We invest heavily in technology infrastructure, content curation, and user experience to ensure that our platform remains at the forefront of the short-form entertainment industry.

As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to our core values and our mission to transform how the world experiences short-form entertainment. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey.
